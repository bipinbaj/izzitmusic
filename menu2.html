About PunjabiJunction

We are a family run restaurant providing comfort and adventure of Indian cuisine away from the native lands.

Comfort paradise for travellers and students, and exciting culinary experience for others.

We started many years ago and made a success story there on. We excel at introducing real taste of India and are extremely well equiped with the knowledge and experience to host big get togethers ans auspicious ceremonies. Call us at 020-817-7370, to book the event and discuss details.
